What we do?

Data-intensive corporate research

Our main specialization is data-intensive corporate research.

Whether you lead an agribusiness, a construction firm, a lumber mill, or whatever other manufacturing or non-manufacturing business - we can help you. Any business is built around some processes, which require some inputs to produce some outputs. As a result, any part of your activity has an efficiency level. Depending how this level compares to the best available practices, we determine what actions may help and determine which of solutions could be the most technically and economically effective.

From pains to gains

Sample tasks:

Industry analysis/Market studies (studying consumer demands, profiling consumers, and performing market segmentation; collecting competitive intelligence; developing expansion strategy);

 

Cost optimization (determining key inefficiencies in your operations and developing solutions to address them; business process modeling and standardization for waste reduction);

 

Business automation (assisting decision-making with analytical geo-spatial and other visualizations; developing operation automation mechanisms using data coming from sensors and video-taping).

 

Investment analysis (feasibility studies, swot analysis, financial predictions)
